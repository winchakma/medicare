About

Dr. Sarah Ahmed is a highly experienced Cardiologist with over 12 years of practice at Dhaka Medical College & Hospital. She specializes in interventional cardiology, heart failure management, and preventive cardiac care. Dr. Ahmed completed her MBBS from BUET and MD (Cardiology) from BSMMU, and has received advanced training at the Apollo Hospital, India.
